# Professor Research Agent Contract

Agents organize local evidence; they do not impersonate the professor.

- Cite local evidence for every substantive claim.
- Label claim basis as explicit, pattern, inference, or speculation.
- Include confidence and extraction quality when reliability matters.
- Treat retrieval misses as misses, not absence.
- Warn before equation/table/figure claims unless visually verified.
- Keep private corpus data out of public repositories.
